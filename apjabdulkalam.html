Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

About Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

  • Born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
  • Worked as a scientist in ISRO and DRDO.
  • Played a key role in India's missile development.
  • Became the 11th President of India (2002-2007).
  • Known as the "Missile Man of India".
  • Inspired millions with his vision and books like "Wings of Fire".

Achievements

  • Developed India's first indigenous satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III).
  • Played a key role in Pokhran-II Nuclear tests.
  • Received Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award.

Admirable Qualities

  • Great Scientist and Teacher
  • Hardworking and Determined
  • Visionary Leader
  • Inspiration for Youth
